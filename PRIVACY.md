# WallSync Privacy Policy

Copyright (c) 2026 Yuri Santos. All rights reserved.

WallSync is designed to run locally on your Mac.

## Data Collection

WallSync does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

## Local Files

WallSync may access video files that you select in order to use them as wallpapers. These files remain on your Mac and are not uploaded by WallSync.

## Analytics and Tracking

WallSync does not include analytics, advertising trackers, or telemetry.

## Network Access

WallSync does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Developer through the official WallSync distribution page.
